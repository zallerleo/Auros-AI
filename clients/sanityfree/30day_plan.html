Before We Start

What I Need From You

To get started, I need the following. Everything else I handle on my end.

Google Account

A Google account tied to the business (or I create a Google Business Profile and add you as owner). Takes 5 minutes.

Website Access

Admin login to sanityfree.cleaning so I can add landing pages, a lead capture form, and a testimonials section.

30-Minute Call

One call to understand your services, service areas, ideal clients, and how your job tracking software works.

Client Contact Method

How you reach clients after a completed job (email, WhatsApp, your software). Needed for the review automation.

Week 1

Goal: Get found on Google

  • Create Google Business Profile — Barcelona. Full setup: business name, address, phone, service areas, business hours, categories (commercial cleaning, janitorial service, office cleaning), and description in Spanish and English.
  • Create Google Business Profile — Buenos Aires. Same setup for the Argentina operation. Separate profile, separate location.
  • Upload photos to both profiles. Minimum 10 photos per profile: team at work, equipment, before/after shots, the SanityFree van/branding if it exists. Google rewards profiles with photos.
  • Add Warner / Harry Potter project as a Google post. This is immediate social proof. "SanityFree provided cleaning and sanitization services for the Warner Bros. Harry Potter experience at Port Vell, Barcelona."
  • Submit for Google verification. Google will verify the business via postcard, phone, or email. This usually takes 3-7 days.

Week 2

Goal: Build trust with reviews

  • Set up automated review request system. After every completed job, the client receives a WhatsApp message or email: "Thanks for choosing SanityFree! If you're happy with our work, a quick Google review helps us a lot: [direct review link]."
  • Seed initial reviews from existing clients. Identify 15-20 current happy clients. Send a personal message from Luciano asking for a Google review. Target: 10-15 reviews in the first two weeks.
  • Create a Google review link shortcut. A simple URL (or QR code) that goes directly to the "Leave a Review" page. Makes it effortless for clients.
  • Set up review monitoring alerts. Get notified whenever a new review comes in so you can respond within 24 hours. Responding to reviews boosts rankings.

Week 3

Goal: Capture and convert leads

  • Build 5-8 local landing pages on the website. Each page targets a specific service + city combination. Examples: "Limpieza de oficinas en Barcelona," "Commercial cleaning Barcelona," "Limpieza industrial Buenos Aires," "Sanitización y desinfección Barcelona." AI-generated content, SEO-optimized.
  • Add a lead capture form to every page. Simple form: name, email, phone, type of space, message. Not "Schedule a Meeting" — just "Get a Free Quote." Lower commitment = more leads.
  • Set up instant auto-response. When someone fills out the form, they get an immediate email or WhatsApp: "Thanks for reaching out! We'll get back to you within 2 hours with a custom quote. In the meantime, could you let us know the approximate size of your space?"
  • Add testimonials section to the website. Pull in the Google reviews as they come in, plus the Warner case study. Social proof directly on the site.
  • Fix website gaps. Add service area pages (Barcelona, Buenos Aires), add FAQ section, consolidate the two website domains.

Week 4

Goal: Automate follow-up & measure results

  • Build lead follow-up sequence. Automated messages for leads that don't respond to the initial quote. Day 2: "Did you get a chance to review our quote?" Day 5: "Happy to do a free walkthrough of your space." Day 10: "We have availability opening up — want us to hold a slot?"
  • Set up tracking and attribution. Every lead gets tagged with where it came from (Google, website, referral). Every conversion gets logged. This is how we prove ROI.
  • Deliver first monthly report. Google Business Profile views, search queries, website traffic, leads received, reviews collected. Baseline established.
  • 30-day review call with Luciano. Walk through results, discuss what's working, plan month 2 priorities.
