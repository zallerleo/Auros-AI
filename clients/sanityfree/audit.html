Finding 01

You Don't Exist on Google

SanityFree has no Google Business Profile for either Barcelona or Buenos Aires. When someone searches "commercial cleaning Barcelona" or "limpieza de oficinas Barcelona," SanityFree does not appear — not in the map pack, not in local results, nowhere.

This is the single most important issue. Google Business Profile is the #1 source of leads for local service businesses. Without it, you are invisible to every potential client who searches for cleaning services in your area.

What happens now

Zero Local Visibility

A potential client in Barcelona searches "limpieza comercial cerca de mi." Google shows a map with 3 businesses. SanityFree is not one of them. That client will never know you exist.

What should happen

Google Maps Presence

A complete, optimized Google Business Profile with correct categories, service areas, photos, and business hours. SanityFree shows up when people search. They call. They book.

Finding 03

Website Looks Professional, But Doesn't Convert

The sanityfree.cleaning website has strong visual design and clean branding. But it's missing the elements that actually turn visitors into leads.

  • No service areas listed — a visitor can't tell if SanityFree operates in their city. You're in Barcelona AND Buenos Aires but the website doesn't say so.
  • No testimonials or case studies — the Warner / Harry Potter exhibition at Port Vell is a major credential. It's not mentioned anywhere on the site.
  • No quick quote form — the only way to contact is "Schedule Meeting" or email. Most leads want to describe their needs and get a response, not commit to a meeting.
  • No pricing or price ranges — despite the company's stated commitment to transparency.
  • No FAQ section — common questions ("Do you bring your own supplies?", "How do you handle keys/access?", "What's your cancellation policy?") go unanswered.
  • Blog inactive since 2024 — signals to both Google and visitors that the site isn't actively maintained.
  • Two websites exist — sanityfree.cleaning and sanityfree.com.ar (listed on LinkedIn). This splits authority and confuses search engines.

Finding 04

No System to Capture or Respond to Leads

Even if someone finds SanityFree, there's no system in place to capture that interest and convert it into a booked job.

Contact options

Email & WhatsApp Only

No lead capture form. No instant response. No automated follow-up. A potential client sends an email and waits. If SanityFree is on a job site, the response could take hours. By then, the client has called a competitor.

Review generation

No System in Place

SanityFree has proprietary job management software — but it's not being used to trigger review requests. Every completed job is a missed opportunity to collect a 5-star review.

The gap between the quality of the business and the quality of its digital presence is the entire opportunity.
