Our Story

A family bakery where Greek tradition meets Southern hospitality

Zukerino is a family-owned bakery on Cotillion Drive in Dunwoody, bringing authentic Greek flavors to Atlanta since we first opened our doors. Our tsoureki bread — flavored with masticha from the Greek islands — has customers driving two hours just to pick up a loaf. Our baklava, kourabiedes, and galaktoboureko are made from recipes that go back generations.

Walk in and Alex will greet you by name. Your kids will get free samples before you even reach the counter. The display case will make you forget whatever diet you were on. That's not a bakery — that's family. We also make American classics: Black Forest cake with real cherries, Napoleon, gingerbread cookies, and custom celebration cakes for every occasion.
