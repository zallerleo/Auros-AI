Recommended Angle: Revenue Engine

The Revenue Engine angle directly addresses the biggest pain point for IP owners: proving ROI on experiential investments. With established partnerships with Universal, Warner Bros, and MGM, The Imagine Team has the credibility to own this positioning. The Harry Potter exhibition's 191K Instagram following demonstrates real audience engagement that translates to revenue. This angle transforms their service from a cost center to a profit driver, making budget conversations easier and creating premium pricing power.

Choose 'End-to-End Authority' if competing primarily against fragmented vendor networks rather than other experiential agencies. Choose 'IP Amplifier' if the primary growth strategy focuses on expanding entertainment industry partnerships over diversifying into corporate and cultural clients.
