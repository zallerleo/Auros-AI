Visual Direction
How every frame should feel.

Photography Style

Moody, intimate, warm practical lighting. Close-ups on cocktail crafting, the vault door moment, hands on vintage artifacts. Shallow depth of field. Every image should feel like a secret being shared.

Video Direction

Slow reveals. ASMR cocktail sounds. Whispered riddles. The moment the vault opens. Camera moves through shadows into golden light. Every cut should feel like uncovering a secret.

Typography

Elegant serif for headlines that feel like invitations to a private club. Gold foil textures on dark backgrounds. Clean sans-serif for practical information. Everything whispers luxury.

Tone of Voice

Mysterious, inviting, exclusive. "You've found it. Most people never do." Speak in second person, like confiding a secret. Never shout. Never explain too much. Let curiosity do the work.

Visual Direction
How every frame should feel.

Photography Style

Cinematic scale, dramatic lighting, architectural majesty. Low angles emphasize grandeur. Warm tones for luxury, cool tones for ocean and drama. Visitors shot as explorers discovering history.

Video Direction

Sweeping crane-style movements. Slow dolly through grand spaces. Orchestral score builds with visuals. Cross-dissolves between historical imagery and exhibition recreations.

Typography

Elegant serif for headlines that evoke the era. Modern sans-serif for CTAs. White and gold on deep blue for the premium, maritime feel. Every word should feel monumental.

Tone of Voice

Reverent but compelling. Cinematic narration style. Facts that stagger: numbers, dimensions, stories. Make history feel urgent: "Before this chance disappears..."

Visual Direction
How every frame should feel.

Photography Style

Wide establishing shots of trolls in nature. Child's POV looking up at giant sculptures. Family adventure moments, kids running toward trolls. Detail shots of recycled materials showing craft and sustainability.

Video Direction

Discovery reveals: turn corner, GIANT TROLL. Drone shots showing scale against the forest canopy. Kids running toward trolls with joy. Seasonal changes: same troll in snow, spring blossoms, summer green, autumn gold.

Typography

Playful serif for headlines that feel like storybook titles. Organic, hand-drawn elements mixed with clean modern type. Earthy tones with pops of bright green and gold. Every word should feel like an invitation to explore.

Tone of Voice

Adventurous, playful, awe-struck. "You won't believe what's hiding in these woods." Speak to families and adventurers. Create a sense of quest. Make every visitor feel like an explorer.
