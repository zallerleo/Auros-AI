# AUROS — Creative Brief
## Client: Entertainment / Exhibitions

---

### Client Overview
- **Industry**: Entertainment — Building Exhibitions
- **Relationship**: Within Leo's direct network
- **Status**: First AUROS client — portfolio showcase before meeting

---

### Campaign Objective
Create a portfolio of spec ads that demonstrate AUROS's ability to produce premium, conversion-ready marketing content for the exhibition industry. The goal is to walk into the meeting and leave no doubt that AUROS delivers world-class work.

---

### Target Audience (Exhibition Visitors)
- **Demographics**: 25-55, urban, culturally engaged, disposable income
- **Psychographics**: Experience seekers, social media active, value unique moments, FOMO-driven
- **Behavior**: Discover events via Instagram/TikTok, buy tickets online, share experiences socially

---

### Key Messaging Themes
1. **Spectacle** — "You've never seen anything like this"
2. **Immersion** — "Step inside, don't just observe"
3. **Exclusivity** — "Limited time. Limited capacity."
4. **Discovery** — "A new way to experience [subject]"
5. **Social currency** — "The experience everyone's talking about"

---

### Tone & Voice
- Cinematic, not salesy
- Mysterious, not vague
- Confident, not arrogant
- Aspirational, not pretentious

---

### Deliverables

#### Video Ads (3)
| Format | Duration | Platform | Priority |
|--------|----------|----------|----------|
| Reels/TikTok | 15 sec | Instagram, TikTok | High |
| Feed/Pre-roll | 30 sec | YouTube, Instagram | High |
| Brand Story | 60 sec | YouTube, Facebook | Medium |

#### Static/Carousel Ads (5+)
| Format | Size | Platform |
|--------|------|----------|
| Square | 1080x1080 | Instagram Feed, Facebook |
| Vertical | 1080x1920 | Stories, Reels cover |
| Horizontal | 1200x628 | Facebook, LinkedIn |
| Carousel (5 slides) | 1080x1080 | Instagram, Facebook |

---

### Visual Direction
- **Lighting**: Dramatic, high-contrast, volumetric light rays
- **Colors**: Rich darks with warm gold accents (aligned with AUROS brand)
- **Scale**: Emphasize the grandeur — wide shots showing the exhibition's scale
- **People**: Silhouettes and crowd reactions — show the emotion, not faces
- **Texture**: Architectural details, materials, interactive elements

---

### Competitive Landscape
- Most exhibitions rely on basic event photography and generic social media posts
- Very few use cinematic AI-generated content for marketing
- **AUROS advantage**: Deliver Hollywood-quality visuals at a fraction of the cost and timeline

---

### Success Metrics (for the meeting)
- Client immediately sees themselves using this content
- Client asks "how did you make this?" (curiosity = trust)
- Client sees the gap between what they currently have and what AUROS delivers
- Meeting converts to a paid engagement
