Visual Direction
How every frame should feel.

Photography Style

Moody, intimate, magic hour, warm practical lighting. Every image should feel like stepping into a film still. Shallow depth of field on details. Wide angles to show scale of sets.

Video Direction

Slow reveals. Camera tracking through spaces like a first-person discovery. Quick cuts for energy, slow motion for awe. Sound design: ambient magic, footsteps, whispered spells.

Typography

Serif (elegant, timeless) for headlines that feel like book titles. Clean sans-serif for CTAs that drive action. Gold text on dark backgrounds for premium moments.

Tone of Voice

Warm but mysterious. Inviting but exclusive. Never corporate. Speak as if revealing a secret world. Use second person: "You'll see... You'll feel... You'll discover..."

Visual Direction
How every frame should feel.

Photography Style

Cinematic scale, dramatic lighting, architectural majesty. Low angles emphasize grandeur. Warm tones for luxury, cool tones for ocean and drama. Visitors shot as explorers.

Video Direction

Sweeping crane-style movements. Slow dolly through grand spaces. Orchestral score builds with visuals. Cross-dissolves between historical and exhibition imagery.

Typography

Elegant serif (historic, grand) for headlines that evoke the era. Modern sans-serif for CTAs. White and gold on deep blue for the premium, maritime feel.

Tone of Voice

Reverent but compelling. Cinematic narration style. Facts that stagger: numbers, dimensions, stories. Make history feel urgent: "Before this chance disappears..."
