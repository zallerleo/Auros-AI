## 1. Google AI Overviews slash search click-through rates

Analysis shows significant CTR declines for queries where AI summaries appear. Fewer clicks mean fewer opportunities to win page rankings. Value now flows to being the source AI cites, not the page that ranks third.

*Source: [Adweek](https://www.adweek.com/brand-marketing/10-ai-marketing-trends-for-2026-agentic-ai-and-search-shifts/)*

## 2. Marketers shift from AI tools to connected workflows

The mental model is changing from 'AI in the stack' to coordinated systems that plan, execute, and optimize campaigns autonomously. Humans supervise. Agents operate. This marks the evolution from discrete point solutions to integrated automation.

*Source: [Adweek](https://www.adweek.com/brand-marketing/10-ai-marketing-trends-for-2026-agentic-ai-and-search-shifts/)*

## 3. AI video tools bridge marketing and learning workflows

Organizations are using AI-powered video solutions for platform-specific social content, eLearning modules, and global translations. The technology is streamlining production from concept to delivery. Speed and personalization at scale are driving adoption beyond marketing teams.

*Source: [PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/ai-tools-are-revolutionizing-video-editing-and-content-creation-for-marketing-and-learning-explains-info-tech-research-group-in-new-report-302361181.html)*

## 4. Multi-modal AI generation eliminates tool switching

Emerging models create text, visuals, and video in one workflow. Marketers can generate complete campaign packages from a single prompt. This consolidation reduces production friction and accelerates testing cycles for performance teams.

*Source: [Funnel.io](https://funnel.io/blog/generative-ai-in-marketing)*

## 5. Only 14% of businesses have complete customer views

Gartner data reveals the customer data gap most brands face. AI-powered 'digital twins' are emerging as the solution, creating simulations that test strategies before real-world implementation. This approach lets marketers identify pitfalls and optimize engagement before campaigns launch.

*Source: [Cork Tree Creative](https://corktreecreative.com/blog/ai-marketing-trends-2026/)*
