# Video Ad Template — 60 Seconds
## Platform: YouTube / Facebook / LinkedIn

### Structure
| Timestamp | Element | Duration | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|----------|---------|
| 0:00-0:05 | **CINEMATIC HOOK** | 5s | Wide shot, dramatic lighting. Set the mood. |
| 0:05-0:15 | **STORY SETUP** | 10s | What is this? Why should I care? Context and intrigue. |
| 0:15-0:35 | **EXPERIENCE** | 20s | Full walkthrough. Multiple angles, reactions, details. The meat. |
| 0:35-0:45 | **EMOTIONAL PEAK** | 10s | The moment that gives chills. Slow motion, music swell. |
| 0:45-0:52 | **PROOF** | 7s | Testimonial, stat, social proof. Ground it in reality. |
| 0:52-0:60 | **CTA + BRAND** | 8s | Urgency + clear action. Client branding. AUROS tag. |

### Production Notes
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 primary for YouTube/LinkedIn, 1:1 secondary for Facebook feed
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 minimum, 4K preferred
- **Music**: Full arc — ambient intro, building middle, peak, resolve to CTA
- **Voiceover**: Recommended for 60s. Script should be 90-100 words max.
- **Color grading**: Warm highlights, deep shadows, gold tones where appropriate
- **B-roll ratio**: 70% experience footage, 20% reaction shots, 10% brand/CTA
- **End card**: 3-5 seconds with logo, website, and primary CTA
