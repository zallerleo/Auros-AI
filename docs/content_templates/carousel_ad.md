# Carousel Ad Template — 5 Slides
## Platform: Instagram / Facebook / LinkedIn

### Structure
| Slide | Element | Purpose |
|-------|---------|---------|
| 1 | **HOOK IMAGE** | Stop the scroll. Bold visual + 3-5 word headline. No logo. |
| 2 | **THE PROBLEM** | What the audience is missing. Create FOMO or curiosity. |
| 3 | **THE EXPERIENCE** | Best shot of what's offered. Immersive, aspirational. |
| 4 | **THE PROOF** | Social proof, stats, testimonial, or behind-the-scenes. |
| 5 | **THE CTA** | Clear action + client branding + AUROS tag. |

### Design Specs
- **Size**: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait, better engagement)
- **Visual continuity**: Each slide should visually connect — same color grading, consistent layout
- **Text hierarchy**: Headline (Inter 700, 32-40px) → Body (Inter 400, 16-20px)
- **Colors**: Midnight (#0B0F1A) backgrounds, gold (#C9A84C) accents, white (#FAFAF8) text
- **Swipe cue**: Subtle arrow or visual element leading to next slide edge
- **Brand placement**: Client logo on slide 5 only. AUROS watermark bottom-right, 8px, 30% opacity.
