# Video Ad Template — 15 Seconds
## Platform: Instagram Reels / TikTok / YouTube Shorts

### Structure
| Timestamp | Element | Duration | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|----------|---------|
| 0:00-0:03 | **HOOK** | 3s | Stop the scroll. Dramatic visual or provocative question. |
| 0:03-0:10 | **SHOWCASE** | 7s | The "wow" moment. Best visual of the experience. |
| 0:10-0:13 | **CTA** | 3s | Clear action: "Book Now", "Learn More", link. |
| 0:13-0:15 | **BRAND** | 2s | Client logo + AUROS watermark (subtle). |

### Production Notes
- **Aspect ratio**: 9:16 (vertical) primary, 1:1 (square) secondary
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 minimum
- **Music**: High energy, matches brand tone. Fade in on hook.
- **Text overlays**: Inter Bold, AUROS gold (#C9A84C) on dark, max 6 words per screen
- **Transitions**: Hard cuts for energy. No gimmicky effects.
- **Hook rule**: First frame must be visually arresting — no logos, no text, pure visual.
