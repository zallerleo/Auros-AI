# Video Ad Template — 30 Seconds
## Platform: YouTube Pre-roll / Instagram Feed / Facebook

### Structure
| Timestamp | Element | Duration | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|----------|---------|
| 0:00-0:05 | **HOOK** | 5s | Problem statement or intrigue. Question the viewer can't ignore. |
| 0:05-0:12 | **REVEAL** | 7s | Show what's possible. First glimpse of the experience. |
| 0:12-0:20 | **SHOWCASE** | 8s | 3-4 quick cuts showing the best moments. Build excitement. |
| 0:20-0:25 | **PROOF** | 5s | Social proof, stat, or urgency element. |
| 0:25-0:30 | **CTA + BRAND** | 5s | Clear call to action. Client logo. |

### Production Notes
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 (landscape) for YouTube, 1:1 for feed, 9:16 for stories
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 minimum
- **Music**: Build from ambient to energetic. Peak at showcase section.
- **Voiceover**: Optional. If used, keep it confident and concise. No filler words.
- **Text overlays**: Reinforce key messages. Inter font family only.
- **Pacing**: Cuts every 2-3 seconds during showcase. Slower for hook and CTA.
