08 — Brand Rules
Do & Don't
What protects the brand and what destroys it.
✓ Do
✓Use gold only as an accent — never fill entire pages with it
✓Write in short, confident sentences — less is always more
✓Lead with numbers and results in all client-facing content
✓Use AUROS in all caps — always
✓Keep whitespace generous — let the name breathe
✓Pair dark backgrounds with gold for premium moments
✗ Don't
✗Never write "Auros" — it's always AUROS, all caps
✗Never use bright colours outside the defined palette
✗Never use buzzwords without data — "cutting-edge," "revolutionary," "game-changing"
✗Never make income promises or use hype language
✗Never stretch or skew the wordmark
✗Never place the logo on cluttered or low-contrast backgrounds
